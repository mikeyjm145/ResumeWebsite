Objectives

1. Find a place where I belong.
I hope to find a work environment I can grow and help others grow, a community/workplace that is a friendly, but strict environment.
2. To become a productive member in any environment.
I hope to gain experience from my work that helps me become a productive member to my current employer and my future employers. I believe the skills I learn and personalities I meet on the job will prep me to handle all types of situations, whether inside or outside of work.
3. To learn from others and teach others along the way.
With the knowledge I gain from working, I will look to share the knowledge among co-workers if it furthers the productivity of the company. I will also seek to learn from co-workers around me to improve my productivity as a worker for the benefit of the company and myself.
